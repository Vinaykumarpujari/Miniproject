NSS SPECIAL CAMP

  • Special camp is a 7 day programme ,which is conducted by nss coordinaters in any particular village.
  • For this special camp nss coordinators select nss volunteers based on their skills,behaviour,performance in the college.
  • These volunteers further participate in special camp by going to any nearer village to the campus, without expecting anything.
  • In these 7 days ,volunteers are involved in seven different activities that would help in knowing some problems in the village.

Day 1

We had a warm welcome by the villagers. Sarpanch looked after our stay whole heartedly.Also they allowed us to use school area for our Special Camp. After arriving to school campus camp setup was done by all volunteers. After arriving school all volunteers made arrangements for the Inaugural Program at school.we started our programme with NSS song.At first cleaning the school area was done in a proper manner and planted trees in school ground .The core area of work was shown to all volunteers while interacting with villagers. Our coordinator Rathna Kumari Challa Mam explained what is NSS? Why we need to work without any expectations? For these questions sir has explained very nicely. Volunteers helped the cooks to prepare the lunch.

Day 2

The second day of NSS camp started with the morning prayer.Today the volunteers processed to clean the school campus and some places in the village. Each and every volunteer participated selflessly in the school cleanliness drive. In the afternoon session our NSS programme officer Rathna Kumari Challa mam shared their experience about NSS.

Day 3

The day started with some discussions as our coordinator divided volunteers into four teams and they allocated different street for all the teams to go for survey regarding no.of people present in a home, problems faced by the villagers.After the completion of surveing volunteers found that there was scarcity in water supply and power supply .

Day 4

The day started with Morning assembly and prayer followed by the attendance of the NSS volunteers. The first activity of third day of NSS special camp was interaction with students, volunteers got to know the difficulties of children who study in village government schools ,and volunteers helped in clearing the doubts of the students in particular subject and also by interacting with them we came to know that some of the students were interested to participate in culturals, so we made some practice sessions for them to participate in the final day cultural event

Day 5

The fifth day was dedicated to plantation programme. On that day there were rally about “Environment Conservation”,in this rally NSS volunteers has conveyed the importance of environment and importance of planting trees, after completion of the rally every volunteer started digging the ground for planting trees and the responsibility of that plant was given to the students of the school.

Day 6

On sixth day volunteers participated in awareness campaign on “Child Marriages”,as we all know that most of the villagers encourage child marriages which is not good for the child and their family because they were not that much matured enough,so volunteers make students as well as parents aware on avoiding child marriages by telling them the problems faced after the child marriage.

Day 7

This is the last day of special camp , half of the day involved in rehearsals and makeup section, after the completion of lunch the validectory event was started. Some volunteers and the students of that school performed several cultural activities like dance,song
